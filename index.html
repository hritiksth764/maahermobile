Heritage · Legacy · Identity

Maaher, a call back to our maternal homes, gives us a chance to deeply explore the tales behind our products — it is our way of reconnecting with a time and space that shaped us.

A Future Helmed By Our Past

Maaher, deeply rooted in Maharashtra's expansive culture, brings the divine luxury of traditionally woven Paithani Sarees — acknowledging what it means to come back home. Surrounded by the legacy forged by our foremothers, we revive and celebrate — with joy, fulfilment, and honour — the bold femininity and graceful audacity.

Our cultural foundation was laid by the women that came before us — we owe it to them to build on it. Our journeys, initiated by mothers and daughters of times gone by, push forth with strength, empathy, and respect.
